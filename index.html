// About Me

Hi, my name is Julian Horvat, I'm a young developer in the second half of his undergraduate degree at the University of Guelph.

I've been using computers my whole life but only discovered programming once I got to highschool, but once I found it I never looked back. My experience is with more traditional languages like C and Java but I've been furiously teaching myself web development in my spare time for the past year. I also love video games and the outdoors!
